 Mr. Speaker, I rise today to pay tribute to a member  of the Greatest Generation, a Montanan whose service to our Nation and  Montana stretched across eight decades.   Born and raised in Butte, Phillip ``Herk'' Lyons answered the call to  serve following the attack on Pearl Harbor. He joined the Navy and  served as a submariner in both World War II and the Korean war.
