 Mr. Speaker, pursuant to clause  2(a)(1) of rule IX, I rise to give notice of my intent to raise a  question of the privileges of the House.   The form of the resolution is as follows:   House Resolution 114.   Resolved, That, pursuant to article I, section 5, clause 2 of the  Constitution of the United States, Representative George Santos, be,  and he hereby is, expelled from the House of Representatives.
