 Madam President, I ask unanimous consent that the cloture  motions with respect to the Perez and Murphy nominations be withdrawn  and that notwithstanding rule XXII, tomorrow at 11:30 a.m., the Senate  vote on confirmation of the Perez nomination and cloture on the Scott  nomination; that if cloture is invoked on the Scott nomination, at 1:30  p.m., the Senate vote on confirmation of the Scott and Murphy  nominations; and that at 5 p.m., the Senate vote on cloture on the  Hopkins nomination and confirmation of the Blackwell nomination;  further, that the cloture motions with respect to the Montgomery-Reeves  and Douglas nominations ripen following disposition of the Hopkins  nomination; and finally, that if any nominations are confirmed during  Wednesday's session of the Senate, the motions to reconsider be  considered made and laid upon the table and the President be  immediately notified of the Senate's action.
