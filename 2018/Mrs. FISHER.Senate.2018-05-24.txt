 Mr. President, I have 6 requests for committees to meet  during today's session of the Senate. They have the approval of the  Majority and Minority leaders.   Pursuant to rule XXVI, paragraph 5(a), of the Standing Rules of the  Senate, the following committees are authorized to meet during today's  session of the Senate:               Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs    The Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs is authorized to  meet during the session of the Senate on Thursday, May 24, 2018, at  9:30 a.m. to conduct a hearing entitled ``Cybersecurity: Risks to the  financial services industry and its preparedness.''                             Committee on Finance    The Committee on Finance is authorized to meet during the session of  the Senate on Thursday, May 24, 2018, at 9 a.m. to conduct a hearing  entitled ``Rural Health Care in American: Challenges and  Opportunities.''                             Committee on Finance    The Committee on Finance is authorized to meet during the session of  the Senate on Wednesday, May 23, 2018, at 10 a.m. to conduct a hearing  on the nomination of John J. Bartrum, of Indiana, to be Assistant  Secretary of Health and Human Services.
