 Mr. Chairman, I oppose this bill  because I believe that it makes it more likely that a future interim  storage site--potentially one in New Mexico--becomes a permanent home  for nuclear waste.   I know that these are tough issues, and I agree that we have a  responsibility to address the waste issues that result from our country  entering the atomic age.   However, addressing nuclear waste is not our only responsibility.  Seventy years ago, rural New Mexico became ground zero for the  detonation of the first nuclear bomb.
