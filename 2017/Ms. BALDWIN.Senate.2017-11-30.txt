 Mr. President, I have a motion to commit at the desk.   The PRESIDING OFFICER. The clerk will report the motion.   The bill clerk read as follows:         The Senator from Wisconsin [Ms. BALDWIN] moves to commit       the bill H.R. 1 to the Committee on Finance of the Senate       with instructions to report the same back to the Senate in 3       days, not counting any day on which the Senate is not in       session, with changes that--        (1) are within the jurisdiction of such committee; and        (2) support the President's plan to close the carried       interest loophole.
