 Mr. Speaker, the Rules Committee issued announcements  outlining the amendment process for three measures that likely will be  before the Rules Committee next week.   An amendment deadline has been set for Tuesday, July 18, at 10 a.m.,  for H.R. 218, the King Cove Road Land Exchange Act; H.R. 2910, the  Promoting Interagency Coordination for Review of Natural Gas Pipelines  Act; and H.R. 2883, the Promoting Cross-Border Energy Infrastructure  Act.
