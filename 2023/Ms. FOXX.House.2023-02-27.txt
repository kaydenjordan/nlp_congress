 Mr. Speaker, I rise to recognize a spectacular company that  is headquartered in North Carolina, Golden Corral.   This year, Golden Corral and its many dedicated employees are  celebrating 50 years of providing Americans across the country with  home-cooked classics at an affordable price.   Mr. Speaker, what makes this company truly exceptional is its  unwavering commitment to serving the communities in which it operates.  From its various fundraisers to support local initiatives to its  longstanding partnership with a national nonprofit to support veterans  and their families, it is abundantly clear that Golden Corral lives and  breathes service.   Congratulations to Golden Corral and its many employees on reaching  such a historic milestone. Here is to many more years of service to  America's countless communities.                            ____________________
