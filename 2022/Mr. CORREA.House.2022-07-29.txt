 Mr. Speaker, today I rise to congratulate Katella High  School in my district for winning the National Green Ribbon Award.   Katella High School is one of 36 high schools nationwide to win this  honor. Katella High School has also been recognized as a California  Green Ribbon School not once but twice; in 2020 earning the bronze  award, and in 2021 earning the silver award.   From a Colonist to the Knights, congratulations. Keep up the good  work.  [[Page H7535]]                                ____________________
