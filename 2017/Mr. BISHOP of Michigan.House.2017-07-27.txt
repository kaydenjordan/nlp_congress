 Mr. Speaker, I rise today to pay tribute and  congratulate Crittenton Hospital as we commemorate its 50th anniversary  in the Rochester, Michigan, community.   Over the past 50 years, Crittenton Hospital has faithfully dedicated  itself to serving the Rochester area community and all of its citizens.  Since opening its doors in 1967, Crittenton Hospital has expanded to  meet the needs of a growing community while keeping pace with advances  in healthcare technology and modern approaches to medicine.   The longevity enjoyed by Crittenton Hospital is a testament to its  unique and enduring impact on our community. Its dedication to our  residents ensures that Crittenton Hospital will continue to serve and  care for patients for many years to come.   Mr. Speaker, I am honored to recognize Crittenton Hospital's 50th  anniversary. I thank Crittenton Hospital for its commitment to the  people it serves and to our entire Rochester area community.                            ____________________
