Madam Speaker, today, I rise to recognize the outstanding life of  service to the community of Mrs. Ruby Thompson Green.   Mrs. Green, for the better half of her 103 years on this Earth,  exhibited true altruism and love for the city of Biloxi.   She was born June 5, 1916, in Ahoskie, North Carolina. She earned her  bachelor of science from Hampton University in Hampton, Virginia.
