 DAVIS of Illinois asked and was given permission to  address the House for 1 minute.)    DAVIS of Illinois. Mr. Speaker, I rise to pay tribute to  Representative Ron Dellums, who, among other things, was known as one  of the most outstanding, articulate, and eloquent persons to ever serve  in this House of Representatives.   He was a political, social activist who used his voice and his  mannerisms to educate millions of people throughout the world about  issues, AIDS, HIV, Free South Africa.   He was an icon. He is an icon and will always be an icon, a great  Member of this body.                            ____________________
