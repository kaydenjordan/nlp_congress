Dear Ms. Johnson: This is to advise you that the unofficial       results of the Special Election held on Tuesday, November 8,       2022 for Representative in Congress from the 2nd       Congressional District of Indiana show that Rudolph (Rudy)       Yakym III received 118.688 of the total number of votes cast       for that office (187,320).        It would appear from these unofficial results that Rudolph       (Rudy) Yakym III was elected as Representative in Congress       from the 2nd Congressional District of Indiana.        To the best of our knowledge and belief at this time, there       is no contest to this election.
