 Mr. President, section 3001 of S. Con. Res. 5, the  fiscal year 2021 congressional budget resolution, allows the chairman  of the Senate Budget Committee to revise the allocations, aggregates,  and levels in the budget resolution for legislation considered under  the resolution's reconciliation instructions. Accordingly, I am revising the  allocations for the reconciled committees and other enforceable  budgetary levels to account for the budgetary effects of the amendment.
