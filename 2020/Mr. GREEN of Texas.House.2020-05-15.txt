 Mr. Speaker, the richest country in the world is  facing a looming humanitarian crisis. Hunger looms large in the land,  Mr. Speaker. This bill provides a $14 billion solution with a  humanitarian response to feed the hungry.   Homelessness looms large. Evictions are afoot.
