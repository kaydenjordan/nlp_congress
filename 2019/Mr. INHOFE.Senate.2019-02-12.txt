 Madam President, as chairman of the Senate Committee on  Armed Services, it is our privilege to pay tribute to Daniel Glickstein  as he prepares to leave his position as a detailee for the Senate  Committee on Armed Services and return to his position as an analyst  for the Government Accountability Office. His contributions to our committee's work have been significant  and highly valued by our staff.
