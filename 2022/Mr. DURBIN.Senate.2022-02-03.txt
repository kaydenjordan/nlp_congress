Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the text of  the bill be printed in the Record.   There being no objection, the text of the bill was ordered to be  printed in the Record, as follows:                                  S. 3574         Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of       the United States of America in Congress assembled,       SECTION 1. LAWRENCE M. ``LARRY'' WALSH SR. POST OFFICE.         (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal       Service located at 303 East Mississippi Avenue in Elwood,       Illinois, shall be known and designated as the ``Lawrence M.       `Larry' Walsh Sr. Post Office''.        (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation,       document, paper, or other record of the United States to the       facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be       a reference to the ``Lawrence M. `Larry' Walsh Sr. Post       Office''.                            ____________________     Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the text of  the bill be printed in the Record.
