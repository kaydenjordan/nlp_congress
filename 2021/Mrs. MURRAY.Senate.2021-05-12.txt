 Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent to print the  following letter in the Congressional Record.   There being no objection, the material was ordered to be printed in  the Record, as follows:       To the Secretary of the Senate:        PN1261, the nomination of Jennifer Ann Abruzzo, of New       York, to be General Counsel of the National Labor Relations       Board, having been referred to the Committee on Health,       Education, Labor, and Pensions, the Committee, with a quorum       present, has voted on the nomination as follows--        On the question of reporting the nomination without       recommendation, 11 ayes to 11 noes.        In accordance with section 3, paragraph (1)(A) of S. Res.       27 of the 117th Congress, I hereby give notice that the       Committee has not reported the nomination because of a tie       vote, and ask that this notice be printed in the Record       pursuant to the resolution.                            ____________________
