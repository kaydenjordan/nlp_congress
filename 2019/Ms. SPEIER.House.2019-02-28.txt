Madam Speaker, today I have the great honor of reading  again along with my colleague, Angie Craig, ``I Am Jazz.'' It is a book  about a transsexual young boy who becomes a young girl.   ``I am Jazz.   ``For as long as I can remember, my favorite color has been pink. My  second-favorite color is silver and my third favorite color is green.   ``Here are some of my other favorite things: dancing, singing, back  flips, drawing, soccer, swimming, makeup, and pretending I'm a pop  star.   ``Most of all, I love mermaids. Sometimes I even wear a mermaid tail  in the pool.   ``My best friends are Samantha and Casey. We always have fun  together. We like high heels and princess gowns, or cartwheels and  trampolines.   ``But I am not exactly like Samantha and Casey.   ``I have a girl brain but a boy body. This is called transgender. I  was born this way.''                            ____________________
