 Mr. Speaker, this morning the Rules Committee issued  announcements outlining the amendment processes for two measures that  will likely be before the Rules Committee next week.   An amendment deadline has been set for Monday, July 17, at 10 a.m.,  for H.R. 806, the Ozone Standards Implementation Act of 2017; and  Monday, July 17, at noon, for H.R. 2997, the 21st Century AIRR Act.   The text of these measures is presently available on the Rules  Committee website.   Feel free to contact me or my staff if we may provide any additional  information.                            ____________________
