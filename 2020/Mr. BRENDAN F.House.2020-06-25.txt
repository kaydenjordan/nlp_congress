 BOYLE of Pennsylvania. Mr. Speaker, coronavirus has  infected our great Nation for months, but racism has infected our  society for centuries. Racism has helped cause disparities in  education, health, housing, and, of course, criminal justice.   Well, today we take a historic step to finally do something about it.  I am proud to support and cosponsor the George Floyd Justice in  Policing Act. It is time. It is long past time that we breathe new life  again into our motto, ``Equal Justice for All.''
