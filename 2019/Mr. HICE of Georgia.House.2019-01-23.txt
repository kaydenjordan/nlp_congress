 ``In all cases affecting Ambassadors, other  public Ministers and Consuls, and those in which a State shall be  Party, the supreme Court shall have original Jurisdiction. In all the  other Cases before mentioned, the supreme Court shall have appellate  Jurisdiction, both as to Law and Fact, with such Exceptions, and under  such Regulations as the Congress shall make.   ``The Trial of all Crimes, except in Cases of Impeachment, shall be  by Jury; and such Trial shall be held in the State where the said  Crimes shall have been committed; but when not committed within any  State, the Trial shall be at such Place or Places as the Congress may  by Law have directed.''   Section 3:   ``Treason against the United States, shall consist only in levying  War against them, or in adhering to their Enemies, giving them Aid and  Comfort. No Person shall be convicted of Treason unless on the  Testimony of two Witnesses to the same overt Act, or on Confession in  open court.   ``The Congress shall have Power to declare the Punishment of Treason,  but no Attainder of Treason shall work Corruption of Blood, or  Forfeiture except during the Life of the Person attainted.''
