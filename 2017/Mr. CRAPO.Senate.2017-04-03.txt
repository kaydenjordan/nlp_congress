 Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the order for  the quorum call be rescinded.   The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.   All time has expired.   The bill was ordered to be engrossed for a third reading and was read  the third time.    Mr. President, I ask for the yeas and nays.   The PRESIDING OFFICER. Is there a sufficient second?   There appears to be a sufficient second.   The bill having been read the third time, the question is, Shall the  bill pass?   The clerk will call the roll.
