 ``The number of Representatives shall not exceed one for  every thirty-thousand, but each State shall have at least one  Representative; and until such enumeration shall be made, the State of  New Hampshire shall be entitled to chuse three, Massachusetts eight,  Rhode-Island and Providence Plantations one, Connecticut five, New-York  six, New Jersey four, Pennsylvania eight, Delaware one,  [[Page H102]]  Maryland six, Virginia ten, North Carolina five, South Carolina five,  and Georgia three.''
