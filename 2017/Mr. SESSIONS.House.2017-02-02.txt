 Mr. Speaker, this afternoon, the Rules Committee issued  an announcement outlining the amendment process for H.R. 428, the Red  River Gradient Boundary Survey Act.   The amendment deadline has been set for Monday, February 6, at 3 p.m.  Amendments should be drafted to the bill as introduced and which can be  found on the Rules Committee website.   Mr. Speaker, please be advised, if there are any questions, Members  may contact me or any member of the Rules Committee staff.                            ____________________
